🎓 Introduction

Welcome to Sant Gadge baba. This Rule Book outlines the expectations, rights, and responsibilities of all members of the university community, including students, faculty, and staff.

2. 📜 Code of Conduct

All students must:

Act with integrity, respect, and professionalism.

Refrain from any form of misconduct, dishonesty, or harassment.

Respect university property and the rights of others.

3. 📚 Academic Policies

Plagiarism, cheating, or academic fraud is strictly prohibited.

Students must submit original work for all assignments and assessments.

Grade appeals must follow the official appeal process within 15 working days.

4. ⏰ Attendance & Class Behavior

Minimum 75% attendance is mandatory per course unless exempted for valid reasons.

Mobile phones must be silenced in class.

Disruptive behavior can result in removal from class and disciplinary action.

5. 📝 Examination Rules

Valid ID must be presented at every exam.

No unauthorized materials allowed (e.g., notes, mobile phones, smartwatches).

Late entry over 15 minutes after exam start is not permitted.

6. 🏫 Campus Facilities Use

Facilities (labs, gyms, halls) must be used responsibly.

Booking is required for group study rooms or event halls.

Vandalism will result in fines or suspension.

7. ⚖️ Disciplinary Process

Complaints or reports of misconduct will be reviewed by the Disciplinary Committee.

Students have the right to a fair hearing.

Penalties include warnings, probation, suspension, or expulsion depending on the severity.

8. 🚨 Safety & Emergency Protocols

Emergency exits must not be obstructed.

Fire drills are mandatory.

All incidents (injury, fire, threat) must be reported immediately to campus security.

9. 💻 Technology Use Policy

University internet is for academic use.

Unauthorized access to systems or data is prohibited.

Cyberbullying or online harassment will lead to disciplinary action.

10. 🤝 Anti-Discrimination & Harassment Policy

Zero tolerance for discrimination based on race, gender, religion, disability, or sexual orientation.

All complaints are handled confidentially.

Support is available via the Student Counseling Center and HR Office.

11. 🎉 Student Organizations & Events

All clubs must be registered with the Student Affairs Office.

Events must be approved 14 days in advance.

Alcohol and illegal substances are banned at all student events.

12. 🏠 Housing & Accommodation Rules

Quiet hours: 10 PM to 7 AM.

No overnight guests without prior approval.

Curfew (if applicable): [Time].

Regular inspections will be conducted.

13. 📖 Library & Resource Center Rules

Silence must be maintained.

Books must be returned on or before the due date.

Misuse of resources can lead to suspension of library privileges.

14. 👔 Dress Code

Students must dress appropriately for academic settings.

Lab attire and ID cards are mandatory during practicals.

Offensive or revealing clothing is not allowed.

15. 📬 Grievance Redressal Mechanism

Students can report concerns via the Student Helpdesk or the online portal.

Complaints are reviewed within 5 working days.

Appeals are allowed if resolution is unsatisfactory.

16. ✏️ Amendments

This Rule Book may be revised as needed. All updates will be communicated via official university channels and the university website.
